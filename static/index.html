The most fun you can have with your whole heart.

Google and the PeaceJam Foundation have come together to launch "1 Billion Acts of Peace," an international movement led by 13 Nobel Peace Laureates designed to tackle the toughest issues facing humanity.

Between now and 2018, we aim to inspire one billion actions addressing the roots causes of the most important problems facing our planet, like rights for women and children, access to clean water for all, and alleviating extreme poverty. Our goal is to create the coolest, most effective, and most fun global citizen's campaign that the world has ever seen!

Cards 4 Peace

These do-it-yourself cards are a way for you to tell us what you will do as your act of peace as part of the 1BAOP campaign. Grab one from your building MK(s) and interoffice your response to Sonia Rao, where we'll use your cards to become a part of a Global Mural for Peace.

Or have a photo op with your completed card on three occasions this week:

  • Monday, November 18th after Meng's Mindfulness talk
  • Thursday, November 21st with Ivan Suvanjieff at YouTube (he'll be roaming around YouTube after he films the 1BAOP YouTube channel's inaugural episode)
  • Thursday, November 21st after TGIF during Gabi's performance on the patio

Want to go paperless? Get a digital DIY card and post your reponse to the 1BAOP G+ page (plus.google.com/+1baop). Or tweet us your completed response @1baop.
